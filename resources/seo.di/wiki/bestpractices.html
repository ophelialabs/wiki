SEO Best Practices

  1. Meta Tags: Use descriptive <title>, <meta name="description">, and Open Graph tags for every page.
  2. Semantic HTML: Structure content with <main>, <nav>, <section>, <article>, and <footer> for accessibility and clarity.
  3. Unique Titles & Headings: Ensure every page has a unique <title> and <h1>.
  4. Sitemap & robots.txt: Maintain /sitemap.xml and /robots.txt at the site root.
  5. Performance: Optimize images, use lazy loading, and minimize CSS/JS for fast load times.
  6. Accessibility: Use ARIA labels, alt text, and keyboard navigation to meet WCAG and Section 508 standards.
  7. Mobile-First Design: Ensure responsive layouts and mobile usability.
  8. Structured Data: Use JSON-LD or microdata for rich snippets (e.g., schema.org).
Remember: SEO is an ongoing process. Regularly audit your site and update best practices as search engines evolve.
