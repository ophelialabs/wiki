Compliance & Policy

  • NIST (National Institute of Standards and Technology):
    • Map SEO-related controls to NIST SP 800-53 (e.g., AU-6 Audit Review, Analysis, and Reporting for monitoring search logs).
    • Ensure all public-facing content is reviewed for sensitive data exposure (see AC-3 Access Enforcement).
  • CISA (Cybersecurity and Infrastructure Security Agency):
    • Follow CISAâ€™s web application security guidance to prevent SEO spam, defacement, and malicious indexing.
    • Monitor for unauthorized changes to SEO-critical files as part of incident response.
  • Policy Integration:
    • Document SEO requirements in organizational policies and standards.
    • Include SEO in regular audits and compliance reviews.
    • Train staff on the intersection of SEO, privacy, and security (e.g., avoid leaking sensitive info in meta tags).
  • Privacy: Ensure search analytics and logs are anonymized and comply with GDPR/CCPA.
Note: Compliance is not a one-time task. Review and update your SEO and security policies regularly.
